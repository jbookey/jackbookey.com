JACK'S BAR MITZVAH PROJECT: "Homeless Care Kits"

Many of us who live in L.A. enounter homeless people almost daily. But we're told that handing over cash is not the best way to suppor them. Instead, Jack decided to create these kits: ziplock bags of useful items you can carry in your car and hand out when approached by someone in need. You can participate in this project by bringing any of the following to the Bar Mitzvah service, and Jack will package and distribute to the community.

- One Size Fits All socks
- Cheese and crackers
- Food gift certificates
- Granola/cereal bars
- Combs
- Tissue packs
- Mints, cough drops, gum
